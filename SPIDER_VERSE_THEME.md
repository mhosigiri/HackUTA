## 🎯 Spider-Verse Design Elements

### Typography
```
Headings:    UPPERCASE, BOLD, 3D SHADOWS
Body text:   SEMIBOLD, HIGH CONTRAST
Labels:      BEBAS NEUE, LETTER-SPACING

```

Borders:     4px solid black (comic style)

Black:            Borders, shadows, text
